⚡ Electron Balance Guide
Each element needs a full outer shell of 8 electrons (or 2 for hydrogen). Elements in groups 1–3 give electrons. Groups 5–7 receive electrons. Group 4 shares. Noble gases (Group 8) are already full — they don't bond. Use the cards below to figure out how many atoms of each element are needed to satisfy both sides.
